It's okay to not be okay

In Vietnam, mental health issues can be considered as a misfortune, or crazy behaviors. 56% Vietnamese from the age of 30 to 55 underestimate or have a wrong assumption on mental illness. It happens in many other places in this world as well.

Here at our Mind Oasis, we encourage you to listen to your mind and take a good care of your mental well-being.

The values that we deliver

Scientific studies and research


Collections of well-known research and studies from reliable sources and high-qualified authors. You can broaden your knowledge about the mental health topics that you are interested in.

Open-minded community


Meeting and discussing the relevant topics with like-minded people and people who experience a specific mental issues. It is a supportive community in which your story is appreciated and understood.

Reliable and professional support


Sometimes you feel like you get stucked, overwhelmed and do not know what to do. We highly recommend to find a professional support. We have contacts with local clinics and hospitals that can help you immediately.
